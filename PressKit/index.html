Description

Four Eyed Cat is a one man studio run by Ashwin Lassay, with the goal to bring joy and excitement to people's lives with small mobile games.

History

The Beginnings

Four Eyed Cat was founded on July 7th 2013. After a stint at business school studying Financial Management, Ashwin decided to do something different and start a carreer as a game developer.

Current project: Kicking Balls

After learning to code, making a couple of prototypes and releasing a small game called fyrkant Kicking Balls came to life. Kicking Balls is a fun physics football game.
