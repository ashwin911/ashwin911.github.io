Hunt Gather Cook

Set up shop in this new town full of survivors of the Ooze pandemic. Cook delicious meals for the locals. Go out into the wild to collect the secret ingredient. Psst it's OOZE don't tell anyone.

Explore the blighted lands, cursed by ooze. Fight off enemies and gather and collect ooze and ingredients for your dishes. Sell delicious meals to the friendly locals since rent is due soon.

Can you survive these cursed ever changing lands and find the rare ingredients for your special meal? Dive down deep into the wilderness and dungeons to unlock the mysteries which flooded the world.
